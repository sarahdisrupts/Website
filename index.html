For Founders

Fuel your vision.
Lead what's next.

Women start nearly half of all new businesses in the US, yet receive less than 2% of venture capital. SheDisrupts exists to close that gap — from your first idea to your first raise and beyond.

The Incubation Lab

From idea to product —
with AI at your side.

Most accelerators give you a 12-week sprint and a demo day. We give you a full-stack ecosystem: an AI agent that acts like your best advisor, hands-on mentorship from founders who've been where you're going, and the infrastructure to actually build.

Whether you're building in AI, robotics, deep tech, or consumer — SheDisrupts gives you the runway to go from zero to fundable.

  • AI-Powered Founder Agent — Personalized guidance from idea validation through investor pitch, available 24/7.
  • Incubation & Lab Support — Structured programs to take your idea from concept to working product.
  • Pitch Coaching — Get investor-ready with coaching built on what top VCs actually look for.
  • Mentor Network — Access advisors who've scaled companies and raised capital across industries.
  • Capital Pathways — Connections to Seed, Series A, and SheDisrupts fund as you grow.
  • SheTalk Visibility — Media features, speaking opportunities, and community amplification.
  • Global Community — A network of founders rising together across borders and industries.

Why Invest Through SheDisrupts

Smart capital. Real returns.
Meaningful impact.

Investing in women isn't philanthropy — it's strategy. Our portfolio is built from founders who've gone through rigorous validation, AI-assisted development, and hands-on mentorship. By the time they reach you, they're ready.

SheDisrupts serves as both a pipeline and a partner — vetting founders, structuring deal flow, and building the relationships that make great investments possible.

About SheDisrupts

Built on one
conviction.

Women who are given real access don't just compete. They lead. SheDisrupts exists to make sure every woman with ambition also has the infrastructure.

Sarah Katz
Founder & CEO, SheDisrupts

The Founder

Operator. Builder.
Disruptor.

Sarah has spent her career at the intersection of strategy, technology, and growth — advising organizations from the Fortune 500 to pre-IPO disruptors, and working alongside CFOs and executive teams navigating high-stakes decisions.

As a Founding Partner at Spaulding Ridge, she built deep credibility in environments where trust is earned through precision, delivery, and judgment. She saw the same pattern everywhere: the barriers that limit innovation are the same barriers holding women back from capital, boards, and influence.

She built SheDisrupts to close that gap permanently — creating the full-lifecycle infrastructure that serious women founders and investors have always deserved.
